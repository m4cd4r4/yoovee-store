Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about YooVee® gloves

What materials are YooVee® gloves made from?

YooVee® Premium Fingerless Gloves are crafted from a blend of breathable, stretchy fabrics including nylon, spandex, and microfiber. The palm area features reinforced padding made from durable synthetic leather for added protection and grip.

How do I determine the right size?

To find your perfect fit, measure the circumference of your hand at its widest point (excluding the thumb). Our size guide is as follows: Small (7-7.5 inches), Medium (7.5-8 inches), Large (8-8.5 inches), and XL (8.5-9 inches). If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

How do I care for my YooVee® gloves?

YooVee® gloves are machine washable on a gentle cycle with cold water. We recommend using a mild detergent and avoiding bleach or fabric softeners. Air dry only - do not tumble dry. For best results and longevity, hand washing is preferred.
