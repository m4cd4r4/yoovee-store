Your Hands Deserve Protection

The importance of hand care and protection in your daily life

Your hands work hard every day—typing, lifting, creating, and caring. Just like your skin, they deserve protection from the elements. Sun exposure, environmental stress, and daily wear and tear can lead to premature aging, dryness, and discomfort. According to the Cancer Council Australia, hands are often overlooked in sun protection routines despite being exposed to UV radiation year-round. That's why YooVee® Premium Fingerless Gloves are designed to shield your hands while keeping you comfortable and stylish.

Did you know that the skin on your hands is one of the first places to show signs of aging? The Skin Cancer Foundation reports that UV exposure, pollution, and even daily chores can take a toll on skin health. Dermatologists recommend protective gear for extended outdoor activities. YooVee® gloves offer a sleek and practical way to protect your skin while allowing full dexterity for work, play, and everything in between.

We often focus on skincare for our face, but our hands need just as much attention. The Australian Hand Therapy Association emphasizes that regular hand care is essential for maintaining mobility and preventing conditions that affect hand function. Moisturizing regularly, applying SPF, and wearing protective gloves can help maintain soft, youthful skin while supporting overall hand health. YooVee® gloves provide an added layer of defense without compromising on comfort or style.

Small habits lead to lasting results. Whether you're working, driving, or enjoying the outdoors, your hands are constantly exposed to UV rays and environmental stressors. According to research highlighted by the Australian Physiotherapy Association, proper hand support during repetitive tasks can significantly reduce strain and fatigue. YooVee® gloves seamlessly integrate into your routine, offering an effortless way to keep your skin looking and feeling its best while supporting proper hand ergonomics.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about YooVee® gloves

YooVee® Premium Fingerless Gloves are crafted from a blend of breathable, stretchy fabrics including nylon, spandex, and microfiber. Our materials are selected based on recommendations from the Australian Hand Therapy Association for breathability and comfort during daily activities.

To find your perfect fit, measure the circumference of your hand at its widest point (excluding the thumb). Our size guide is as follows: Small (18-19 cm), Medium (19-20.5 cm), Large (20.5-22 cm), and XL (22-23 cm). If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

YooVee® gloves are machine washable on a gentle cycle with cold water. We recommend using a mild detergent and avoiding bleach or fabric softeners. Air dry only - do not tumble dry. For best results and longevity, hand washing is preferred. Regular washing helps maintain the protective properties of your gloves. The Cancer Council Australia recommends regularly washing sun-protective garments to maintain their effectiveness.
